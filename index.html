Lawn Mowing Tips for Capalaba Residents

  • Don’t cut grass too short—it weakens the turf.
  • Mow when dry to avoid clumping.
  • Alternate directions each session to prevent ruts.
  • Mulch clippings to recycle nutrients.

When to Book Lawn Mowing

During warmer months, mowing is usually needed every 1–2 weeks. In cooler seasons, every 3–4 weeks may be sufficient. A reliable provider can help determine a schedule that keeps your lawn thriving all year.
